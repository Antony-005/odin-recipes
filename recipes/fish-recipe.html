Wet Fried Fish recipe

Description

A simple recipe for a Kenyan meal, mostly common on the lakeside, that is lakes Victoria. It's a common delicacy that is well known to be eaten by 'ugali'

Ingredients

  • Deep fried tilapia
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 2 onions
  • ginger and garlic paste
  • bell papers
  • parsley
  • salt
  • cooking oil
  • 1/2 cup of water

Steps for cooking

  1. Add cooking oil to a pre-heated pot
  2. Add in the onions to cook until golden brown in color
  3. Add the ginger and garlic paste and cook for a few seconds
  4. Add the chopped tomatoes and saute untill broken down
  5. Add the well chopped bell papers and saute for 30 seconds
  6. Add a sprinkle of salt to the mixture and saute
  7. Add 1/2 cup water and bring the mixture to boil
  8. Dip the deep fried fish into the mixture and ensure it is well covered on both sides
  9. After 4 minutes, sprinkle your parsley and turn off the heat
  10. Serve with hot 'ugali' and veges
